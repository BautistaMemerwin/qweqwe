Shake Recipe



avocado
Prepare time
10 mins
Ready in
20 mins

         SERVES: 5

Ingredients

2 c. avocado
1 c. milk
1 whole avocado

Directions

1. Put avocado in a cup and add a little milk (for thicker) and more milk (for less thick). 2. Mix with a spoon until its smooth. If your cup is not filled to your desire add more avocado and milk. 3. Crush avocado into a desired size. Put into milkshake and stir until thoroughly mixed in. 4. Add anything else to the top such as "magic shell" or whipped cream and sprinkles
